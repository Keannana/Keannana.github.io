Adobe Illustrator Project

The character I drew in this project is from a very popular anime called Fairy Tail. His name is called Happy. I chose to illustrate him because I wanted to do something simple, but not too simple. I was sitting in my room one day, and saw him in one of my posters and I realized his design was perfect, so I decided to draw him! I was going to include his friend in the picture too, but was unable to do so due to time constraints, but I think it still came out really nice!
